## Executive Summary

**Orbit** is a decentralized subscription payment platform built on the Stellar blockchain, enabling businesses to accept recurring cryptocurrency payments with minimal fees and instant settlement.

---

## üéØ Problem Statement

### Current Pain Points in Subscription Payments:

1. **High Transaction Fees**
   - Credit cards charge 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
   - PayPal charges 3.49% + fixed fee
   - Stripe charges 2.9% + $0.30

2. **Slow Settlement**
   - Traditional payments take 2-7 business days
   - Chargebacks can occur up to 120 days later
   - International transfers take 3-5 days

3. **Geographic Restrictions**
   - Many payment processors don't serve developing countries
   - Currency conversion fees add 2-4%
   - Banking requirements exclude the unbanked

4. **Centralized Control**
   - Accounts can be frozen without warning
   - Funds can be held for "review"
   - Platform dependence creates risk
