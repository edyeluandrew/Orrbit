### What You Need

1. **A Stellar Wallet** - We recommend [Freighter](https://freighter.app/)

1. Go to [freighter.app](https://freighter.app/)

4. Create a new wallet or import existing

## üí≥ Making a Payment

### Step 1: Select a Service

1. Go to the **Payments** tab
2. Browse available services
3. Click on the service you want

### Step 2: Review Payment

- Check the service name
- Verify the amount in XLM
- See the USD equivalent

### Step 3: Confirm Payment

1. Click **"Pay Now"**
2. Freighter will open with transaction details
3. Review the destination and amount
4. Click **"Approve"**

### Step 4: Done!

- Transaction confirms in ~5 seconds
- You'll see a success notification
- The subscription appears in your history

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my money safe?

Yes! Orbit is **non-custodial** - we never hold your funds. All transactions are signed by you in your own wallet.

### What are the fees?

- **Network fee:** ~0.00001 XLM (negligible)
- **Platform fee:** 2% (included in displayed price)

### How fast are payments?

Stellar transactions confirm in **3-5 seconds**. Your balance updates immediately after confirmation.

### Can I get a refund?

Contact the service provider directly. Orbit facilitates payments but doesn't control refund policies.

### What if a payment fails?

- Check your balance is sufficient
- Ensure your wallet is connected
- Try again in a few seconds
- Contact support if issues persist

### Is this real money?

- **Testnet:** No, it's fake test XLM for development
- **Mainnet:** Yes, real XLM with real value
